I am an assistant professor at Özyeğin University, Computer Science Department. My research activity focuses primarily on Bayesian inference of deep neural nets, transfer learning, meta-learning, few-shot learning, reinforcement learning, and application of these approaches to computer vision and medical image analysis problems.
